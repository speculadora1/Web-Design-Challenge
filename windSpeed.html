Latitude vs. Maximum Temperature

Here we see there is no relationship between the latitude of a city and the wind speed that it experiences this time of year. As latitude approaches the extreme in either direction there do seem to be more cities with high wind speeds, but they are far out-numbered by cities experiencing something normal relative to the average wind speed for the data set.
